Our Services

Every recruitment process is different. You may need a permanent employee or a seasonal worker. We deliver both. Understanding our clients’ needs requires specific knowledge. That’s why we are divided into Teams. Each of them is responsible for recruiting in different sector. By maintaining a specialist focus, our consultants build an in-depth knowledge of their products and their markets. That let’s us deliver services on a highest level, while maintaining competitive prices.

See what each of our Teams can do for you:

How we work

Delivering workers matching our clients requirements is our priority. See below how the whole process looks.

  • Clinet’s Environment Analysis

    • The more we get to know about our client, the more we can do for them. Analyzing client’s environment let’s us understand their needs better.

  • Vacancy Qualification

    • Seemingly the same vacancies may be very different in various comapnies. That’s why we qualify each of vacancies seperately.

  • Candidate Search

    • From social media to extensive database of candidates. We know the sources of candidates.

  • Candidate Introduction

    • Last but not least part of the proccess. We arrange meetings at the office or online. Final decision is always up to our client.
